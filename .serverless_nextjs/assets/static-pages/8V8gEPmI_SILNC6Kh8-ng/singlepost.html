Elizabeth Warren Calls for US to Create a CBDC

I think its time for us to move in that direction, the Democratic senator told NBCs Chuck Todd in an interview to be aired Thursday night.

By Greg Ahlstrand

Oct 18, 2019

Updated Apr 1, 2022 at 8:39 a.m.

U.S. Sen. Elizabeth Warren (D-Mass.) says its time for the U.S. to create its own central bank digital currency (CBDC). Warren spoke with NBCs Chuck Todd on Meet the Press Reports, scheduled to air at 10:30 p.m. ET on Thursday. NBCUniversal shared a partial transcript of the conversation with CoinDesk.

  • So a lot that banks do wrong, if you think, We could improve that in a digital world, the answer is, Sure you could. But in that case, lets do a central bank digital currency, Warren told Todd. Yes, I think its time for us to move in that direction.
